About GIAS

Professional, trustworthy, and student-first education.

Founder’s Profile

Dr. Ayesha Mehra is a visionary healthcare educator with over two decades of experience in academic leadership, clinical training, and curriculum design. Her mission is to ensure every student receives mentorship, practical exposure, and a values-driven learning journey.

She founded GIAS to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world clinical practice, enabling graduates to be confident, compassionate professionals.

Programs & Courses

Career-ready programs designed with modern healthcare needs in mind.

Nutrition

Comprehensive curriculum covering human anatomy, clinical nutrition, and personalized diet planning.

  • Courses Start: Diploma & Degree
  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 Science
  • Highlights: Lab-based learning, hospital diet planning, wellness coaching.

Nursing

Focus on patient care, medical ethics, clinical training, and hands-on hospital experience.

  • Courses Start: B.Sc & GNM
  • Duration: 3-4 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 Science
  • Highlights: Simulation labs, ward rotations, mentorship program.
